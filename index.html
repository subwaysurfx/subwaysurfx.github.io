How to Play Unblocked Subway Surfers?

While playing Subway Surfers game, you move your character left and right or up and down using "W, A, S, D" and arrow keys on computers. On mobile phones and mobile tablets, you perform the same operations by dragging your finger left and right or up and down on the screen. When your character moves up, it jumps. When it moves down, it leans.

The track consists of a 3-lane city traffic. A child playing football in the neighborhood accidentally breaks a neighbor's window. The neighbor gets angry and calls the police, and the police chase after the child to catch him. The child runs in the city to avoid being caught by the police. You help the child and help him not to be caught by the police.

While running in the city of Subway Surfers, you come across cars and barriers that are either flowing or stationary in traffic. If you hit these barriers, you fall to the ground and get caught by the police. To do this, you must escape from the police by moving left and right or up and down. You earn points by collecting the coins on the road. You pull the coins towards you by taking the magnets. This way you can earn a lot of money in a short time.

In Subway Surfers, there are subway, amusement park and airport sections other than the city center. You unlock the subway section when you find 30 jejons in the city center. You unlock the airport when you find 50 dinosaurs in the subway. You move on to the next section when you find 70 planes in the airport.

You can buy a taxi or a faster start feature from the UPGRADE section using the money in your safe. You can buy features such as extending the duration of the magnet feature, running faster and flying. You can buy a new character and change your character. You can buy different and eye-catching skateboards. You can add features such as lying, jumping and turning on the skateboard to your character. Thus, you can play the subway surfers game by advancing longer on the track in your next attempts. We wish you to have fun with this application, every moment of which is full of adventure and excitement.
